Below, please find a high-level agenda of activities and events. Check back for updates!

CAN I EXTEND MY STAY?

Yes! However, before you do, be sure to register your trip through the ZipRecruiter registration page and wait until you receive a confirmation email from the resort (this could take 1-2 weeks as all registrant information is sent to the resort on scheduled dates.) At that time, simply call the Terranea Resort and mention that you recently booked a stay for a ZipRecruiter Client Event and provide the additional dates you would like to extend. The phone number to call is (855) 938-4047. Please note, availability and same room rates are not guaranteed.
